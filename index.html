Changemakers 16 years old and older

Change the world, open your world. Become a change maker youth. Make an impact on the world with your knowledge, drive, and care. You can make a difference.

We strive to open the eyes of our youth by providing opportunities for them to do international volunteer work. We hope that even one trip would lead them on a road to become a social change maker.

We currently focus on 3 things: 1. Match volunteers with international opportunities. 2. Provide a means for donors to specificallly apply their donated funds to a particular need. 3. Provide a community forum for change makers to grow.

FAQ

Can adults supervise a trip with their child?
All trips are supervised by adults. We also welcome parents, relatives, any adult volunteers. However, our main goal is to provide a phenomenal opportunity for the young. We envision the long term effects of global volunteering profoundly changing the mindset of our young. We understand that everyone will not become a change maker. But we hope everyone will be socially aware responsible adults in the future.
What can donors do?
We envision our future change maker to also help raise money for volunteer quest. Donors can earmark their donations to a specific change maker, trip or item. Donors can also do traditional donations.
